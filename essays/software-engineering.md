## The Initial Existential Crisis

I admit that when I first enrolled in the Computer Science program at UH Manoa, I had no idea what my exact career goals were. Computer Science has interdisciplinary collaborations with virtually every field that uses technology. Biology? Bioinformatics. Art? UX Design. Psychology? Cognitive Science. Unlike my usual lecture halls, the flipped classroom model that is particularly popular in computer science classes encouraged me to research different fields of interest and incorporate my findings in the form of personal projects and reflections. The freedom I was given to choose my path in life was both intoxicating and overwhelming.
